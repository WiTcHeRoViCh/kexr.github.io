About


KEXR is a Discord server disguised as a terrestial radio station (it isn't one).

We play classic alternative rock from the 90s and late 80s and reminisce about old games. We run a FocaBot and a relayed audio stream that can be listened to outside of Discord.

Think of KEXR as a bar with a jukebox.
